Arcade Hitbox Creator

  • - Choose a frame, change the zoom if necessary (you can't change it after image opened).
  • - Choose your shape and click the left top start point of the desired hitbox.
  • - Move the mouse to size the hitbox, and click again to stop.
  • - Add more hitbox if needed, or use cancel to try again.
  • - Choose a new frame, and repeat the operation.
  • - Once finished, click Generate Json.
